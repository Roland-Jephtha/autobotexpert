Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our platform and AI trading

How do I set up an AI trading bot?
+
Navigate to the AI Trading section in your dashboard, choose a strategy (DCA, Grid, Arbitrage), set your parameters (investment amount, risk level, target coins), and activate the bot. Our AI will execute trades automatically 24/7 based on market conditions.
How long does account verification take?
+
Standard verification typically takes 10-30 minutes using our AI-powered verification system. Enhanced verification for higher limits may take 24-48 hours. You'll receive an email notification once approved.
What are your trading fees?
+
Our fees start at 0.1% per trade for regular trading. AI bot trades have the same fee structure. We offer volume-based discounts: 0.08% for traders with $100K+ monthly volume, and 0.05% for institutional traders with $1M+ monthly volume.
Are my funds safe with AI trading bots?
+
Yes! Your funds remain in your account at all times. AI bots only execute trades‚Äîthey cannot withdraw funds. You maintain full control and can stop any bot instantly. We also implement stop-loss mechanisms and risk management protocols to protect your capital.
Can I customize my AI trading strategy?
+
Absolutely! Our advanced users can create custom strategies using our Strategy Builder. Set your own indicators, conditions, risk parameters, and backtesting periods. You can also clone and modify existing high-performing strategies from our marketplace.
What happens if the AI makes a wrong prediction?
+
Our AI uses advanced risk management including automatic stop-losses, position sizing, and diversification. While no system is perfect, our algorithms are designed to minimize losses and protect your capital. You can also set maximum loss limits per trade or per day.
